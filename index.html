About

I'm a multi-skilled software developer, UI/UX designer, and IT manager with over 4 years of hands-on experience across digital product design, front-end development, and IT systems.

With a strong foundation in Computer Science and a passion for innovation, I've had the privilege to lead and contribute to impactful projects—ranging from e-commerce platforms and humanitarian tech to digital marketing campaigns and certificate management systems. I currently serve as an IT Specialist & Data Analyst at the South African Red Cross Western Cape, while also running my own software company, LYGM Group, focused on AI Solutions for Automation & Cybersecurity & Compliance

What drives me is solving real-world problems through intuitive design, clean code, and smart systems. Whether it's building full-stack applications with the MERN stack, automating workflows using Python, or designing engaging user experiences, I take pride in delivering work that is both technically sound and people-centered.

I'm always eager to collaborate with like-minded individuals and organizations who believe in the power of technology to create meaningful change. Let's connect, build, and make a difference—one solution at a time.
